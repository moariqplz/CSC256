## How to Use
1. **Add a Task**:
   - Enter a task in the input field and click the "Add Task" button to add it to the list.

2. **Display Tasks**:
   - Click the "Display Tasks" button to refresh and display all tasks in the list.

3. **Toggle Task Completion**:
   - Click on a task in the list to toggle its completion status. Completed tasks will have a strikethrough effect.

4. **Clear Completed Tasks**:
   - Click the "Clear Completed Tasks" button to remove all tasks marked as completed.
